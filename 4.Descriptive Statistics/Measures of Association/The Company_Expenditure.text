The "Company_Expenditure.csv" data is a completely fabricated data set for use only on the upGrad platform.

Any resemblance to entities past, present or future is merely a coincidence.

Feature Description:
Month = month for which expenditure and profit values are recorded
Marketing = amount spent by the company on marketing expenditures in rupees for a specific month
Human Resources = amount spent by the company on human resources expenditures in rupees for a specific month
Infrastucture = amount spent by the company on infrastructure expenditures in rupees for a specific month
